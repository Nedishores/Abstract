Contact Support

  1. While signed in to Abstract, select the Support icon.
    • If you're in the Abstract web app, the Support icon is in the lower
      left corner.
    • If you're in the Abstract Desktop app, the Supporticon is in the
      upper right corner.
  2. Select Contact Support.
  3. On the Contact Support page, choose the Topic you want to
    contact Support about, write a Subject for your message, and enter
    a Description of your issue.
  4. If you have files you want to give Support, such as bug reports or
    screenshots, click Browse files... and choose them from your
    computer.
  5. When you're done, click Send to Support.

Ask questions about subscriptions and billing

Admins can manage and update billing information on behalf of
their Organization directly from the web app, including adding
more contributor seats, managing billing details, and locating
invoices.

For security purposes, Support can only assist with billing matters if you
are an admin or can provide credit card information to verify your identity.
If you want to use card information to verify your identity, you need to
provide:

  • The name on the credit card on file
  • The last four digits of the card number
  • The card expiration date
  • A screenshot of the last charge that Abstract made to the card

If you aren't an admin and can't provide the card information mentioned
above, locate your admin within the web or desktop application. Have this
person contact Support with your questions.
